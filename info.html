About

The Blank Traveler is a clothing brand/label/line that currently specializes in graphic based apparel. That does not, however, mean that it is somehow limited to only graphic based apparel and that more customized cut and sew (fashion term) products will never be made. It mostly means that, as of now, the creator/director/editor of the brand is interested in graphic-based design (not to be confused with Graphic Design). It also means that the creator is also interested in what he likes to refer as readymade blank garments, specifically GILDAN® products (subject to change).

The Blank Traveler products are self printed with direct-to-garment printers in Cambridge, MA and have been since its inception in 2016.
